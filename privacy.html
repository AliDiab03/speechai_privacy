Privacy Policy
Last Updated: June 2026

Thank you for using Speech Notes AI. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We keep things simple: we don't sell your data, and your voice recordings stay on your device.

Information We Collect

We collect only what's needed to run and improve the app:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version and performance data
  • Advertising ID (used by AdMob for ads)
  • Anonymized usage analytics
  • Crash reports

No account required. We don't collect your name or email.

Voice & Transcription Data

Your recordings and transcripts are processed on your device. We don't store or have access to your audio or transcript content. When online transcription is used, audio is sent securely and not retained after processing.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, device info, and ad interaction data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data is shared only with these services to operate the app:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase
  • Google Play Services

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Security

We use HTTPS encryption and standard security practices. No system is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your data.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the date at the top of this page.
